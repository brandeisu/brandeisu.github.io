Lilly Hecht - Associate Justice - Class of 2018
lohecht@brandeis.edu

Lilly is one of the five Justices on the Student Union Judiciary (the branch of the Union that interprets Brandeis' Constitution and By-Laws to neutrally and confidentially arbitrate disputes). She is a junior majoring in English and minoring in Legal Studies and Hispanic Studies. Outside of the Union, she serves as a Legal Studies Undergraduate Department Representative and has been involved with the Undergraduate Theater Collective as a Director, Assistant Music Director, actor and now Secretary of the musical theater club, Tympanium Euphorium. She absolutely loves being part of the Judiciary and REALLY hopes you reach out to us so we can do our job and help you! Throughout her time serving the student body via the Union, she hopes to promote intersectionality, safe spaces, responsible budget-balancing, and increased awareness among students that the Union is here to serve you and truly wants to make our community as supportive of every student as possible!
