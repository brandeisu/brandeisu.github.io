Andrews Figueroa - A-Board member - Class of 2019
andrews9008@brandeis.edu

Andrews is a sophomore majoring in computer science & linguistics; He is also interested in graphic design and film. On campus, he serves as a community advisor for North Quad, is an advisor for the NQ community council, and is the President of Deis Robotics. As part of the Student Union, Andrews holds a three-semester seat on the Allocations Board. Throughout his time in the Union, Andrews will work to respond in a logical and efficient manner to club concerns and will help ensure that they are funded in a just manner. His office hours are Tuesdays 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM and Fridays 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM. Please feel free to reach out to Andrews through the email provided above.



Emily Levine - Treasurer - Class of 2018
ehlevine@brandeis.edu

Emily is a senior double majoring in Business and Economics. She was an Assistant Treasurer her sophomore year and a Deputy Treasurer her junior year. In addition to being the Treasurer for Student Union, she is on the E-board of Chabad at Brandeis and a project manager for TAMID Group at Brandeis. She also interns at a financial management firm in Newton. Emily loves hanging out with her friends and family. When she's not in class or the Student Union room, you can probably find her on the second floor of the library, by Louis' Deli, or on Newbury Street. She enjoys being apart of Student Union and seeing what all the clubs are up to on the Brandeis.



Alexander Feldman - A-Board Chairperson - Class of 2019
felday@brandeis.edu

Alex is a junior studying computer science, gender studies, and economics. Fall 2017 is his 5th semester on A-Board and he is serving as chair. In the spring, he is sailing off to London and we'll miss him dearly. If you have any issues send him an email at felday@brandeis.edu.



Yaoyao Gao - A-Board member - Class of 2020
yaoyaogao@brandeis.edu

Yaoyao is a sophomore intending on majoring economics and still deciding. She is also an assistant treasurer on the E-Board of Brandeis Asian American Student Association. As part of the Student Union, Yaoyao holds a three-semester seat on the Allocations Board. She looks forward to become more involved in club affairs and garner relative experiences in order to contribute to the decision making of allocating funds.



Winnie Zhao - A-Board member - Class of 2020
