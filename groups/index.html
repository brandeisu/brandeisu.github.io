List of Groups


The student Union has a number of branches, working groups and committees. Each group has a list of members, conveniently organizing their contact information in a single place. For more information about a group, reach out to its leaders.


  • Allocations Board - Group Page - Members
    Description: The Allocations arm of the Brandeis Student Union allocates approximately 1.7 million dollars to different areas of campus life. The allocations process can be complicated, so always make sure you follow their established protocol.

  • Bylaws Committee - Group Page - Members
    Description: The Bylaws committee is working to clarify and streamline the operations of the Student Union.

  • Campus Operations Working Group - Group Page - Members
    Description: The COWGI committee deals with campus issues including housing and physical facilities.

  • Executive Board - Group Page - Members
    Description: The Executive Board of the Brandeis Student Union

  • Financial Audit Working Group - Group Page - Members
    Description: Working to better allocate our resources, avoiding fraud, duplication costs, and misuse. Chaired by Emily Conrad '17 and David Herbstritt '17.

  • Judiciary - Group Page - Members
    Description: The Judiciary is the impartial arbiter of disputes within the Student Union. It is comprised of five associate justices, who elect amongst themselves a chief justice.

  • Senate - Group Page - Members
    Description: The Student Union Senate, comprised of 22 senators from all quads and class years, led by the Executive Senator and the Vice President. The Senate Meets every Sunday at 7PM in the Shapiro Campus Center Multi Purpose Room.

  • Treasury - Group Page - Members
    Description: The Treasury of the Brandeis Student Union

The Union Office

Feel free to visit us on the third floor of the Shapiro Campus Center! Our office is a great place to come study, ask questions, and talk about your vision for Brandeis. Check out the full list of office hours to see when you can drop by!
