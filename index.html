Your Student Union

We are here to serve you from orientation to graduation and beyond. How?

Advocacy

We are your link to change. No matter your concern or idea, we have connections within the alumni, administration, faculty, and trustees to address your concerns and ideas. The Executive-Board has strong relationships with all of the Brandeis constituencies, and makes sure that the student voice is heard.

Allocations

The Student Union fills our campus with life through the financial support of our vibrant clubs and associations. The Allocations Board makes all funding decisions for our student run organizations, trying to maximize the total impact of each organization on campus life.

Community

We take pride in our community, and in its stewardship. The Student Union works to promote a safe, respectful, and intentional environement for the student body, creates and reviews student focused laws and initiatives, and builds community within and around our campus.

How Can We Help?

Office Hours

Come by the SCC on the third floor to talk to us about your idea/goal/complaint/vision. Every Union Member spends some time every week in the office, on this page you can find the full list of office hours.

A-Board + Treasury Rules

Make sure you are in the know, and have all the information  you need to request and get funding for your club.

Romper Room

The romper room is a resources for clubs, and has everything they could ever need: iMacs, printing, and office supplies. Request access here.

Elections

Join us!  Click here to find out about the upcoming rounds of elections, and how you can participate!

The Union Office

Feel free to visit us on the third floor of the Shapiro Campus Center! Our office is a great place to come study, ask questions, and talk about your vision for Brandeis. Check out the full list of office hours to see when you can drop by!
